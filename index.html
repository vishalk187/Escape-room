Our Story

Escape Room was founded with a simple vision: to create a perfect space where you can enjoy premium coffee, delicious food, and a cozy atmosphere.

We carefully select the finest ingredients for our menu, ensuring exceptional quality and taste in every item we serve - from our specialty coffees to our signature thickshakes.

Our skilled baristas and chefs are passionate about creating memorable experiences through innovative recipes and combinations that will delight your taste buds and keep you coming back.
